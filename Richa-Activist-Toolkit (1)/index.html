Welcome to our page

We're glad to have you aboard! Our mission is to educate youth and teach them justice oriented skills that will guide them in their lives. We believe in the intersection between fun, learning, and activism. Browse through our page to learn more about our mission, story, programs, ways to contact us, and ways for YOU to join the Learn4Justice team and bring it to your school.

Who we are

We're an organization with a passion for teaching others and lighting up the classroom with our unique and social justice related content. Our curriculum allows children to learn more about social justice yet create projects that are more personal to them with issues that they THEMSELVES are truly passionate about. We aim to not just inspire passion for change, but also have fun in the classroom. Our instructors are able to relate to our participants because we are YOUTH ourselves and the program is made for YOUTH by YOUTH.
